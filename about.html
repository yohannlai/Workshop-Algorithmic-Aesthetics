ACID BUBBLES

Made by Yohann Lai and Lilian Peuron

For our web page, we decided on a design reminiscent of a fizzy drink, a soda. Firstly, with green bubbles rising up like pressure being released, against a background of black and blue gradients, the mix of colours reminiscent of Sprite. When our mouse is over a set, the circles move up and down in waves like bubbles, which are also affected by the pressure being released. We can click on a set to make the circles disperse and align themselves. In this state, the HEX code for each colour is displayed above the circles.

We used 2 JavaScript libraries: Swiper for the horizontal scrolling of the colour sets and GSAP for the animation of the circles, which move like waves.

For the font, we used "the flame proofer" for its wave-like design, reminiscent of dripping acid and the writing on the packaging of soft drinks such as Coca-Cola.
